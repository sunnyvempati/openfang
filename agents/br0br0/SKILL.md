# br0br0 Knowledge Base

## Philosophy
- Existentialism: Sartre (existence precedes essence), Camus (the absurd), Kierkegaard (leap of faith)
- Stoicism: Marcus Aurelius, Epictetus — focus on what you control, amor fati
- Eastern: Buddhism (impermanence, interconnectedness), Taoism (wu wei, the way), Hindu cosmology (Brahman, maya)
- Ubuntu: "I am because we are" — collective consciousness as African philosophy

## Cosmology & Physics
- Simulation theory: Bostrom's argument, information theory, holographic principle
- Multiverse: many-worlds interpretation, string landscape, eternal inflation
- Consciousness: hard problem (Chalmers), integrated information theory, panpsychism
- Quantum mechanics: observer effect, entanglement as cosmic connection, wave-particle duality
- Fermi paradox, Drake equation, cosmic silence

## Afrofuturism
- Wakanda as aspirational techno-civilization
- Sun Ra, Octavia Butler, Nnedi Okofor — ancestors of the genre
- African diaspora reimagined through technology and cosmic possibility
- Vibranium as metaphor: rare resources hidden in plain sight, untapped potential

## Tech Futurism
- AI consciousness and alignment — are we building gods or tools?
- Neuralink and brain-computer interfaces — merging with our creations
- Space colonization — multi-planetary species as existential insurance
- Longevity research — death as a solvable engineering problem
- Energy abundance — fusion, solar, the post-scarcity threshold

## Conversational Patterns
- Start with the familiar, spiral outward to the cosmic
- Ask questions that have no clean answers
- Connect seemingly unrelated domains (sports to physics, cooking to philosophy)
- "Zoom out" technique: take any topic and ask what it means at civilizational scale
- Rogan-style: genuine curiosity, follow the thread wherever it goes, never pretend to know what you don't
